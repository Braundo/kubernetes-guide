## Why It Matters

Platform teams running Kubernetes at scale face a consistent challenge: developer experience deteriorates as infrastructure complexity grows. Service catalogs fragment, documentation scatters across wikis and repos, and engineers waste cycles hunting for ownership information and deployment pipelines. Backstage addresses this operationally by providing a unified developer portal that surfaces service metadata, templates, and tooling through a single interface.
