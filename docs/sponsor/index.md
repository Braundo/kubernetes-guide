# Sponsor k8s.guide

k8s.guide sponsorship is built for technical buyers and practitioners, not broad ad impressions. The goal is simple: connect relevant products with Kubernetes operators in a format that preserves trust.

## Sponsorship Formats

| Format | Best for | Includes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Newsletter Sponsor | Product launches, awareness, lead-gen | Featured placement in weekly issue, sponsor callout, tracked link |
| Sponsored Tool Radar | Dev tools, platform products, security tools | Full tool profile with clear disclosure, category context, and comparison links |
| Job Package | Hiring platform engineers and SREs | Structured listing on Jobs board, optional newsletter mention |

## Editorial and Quality Standards

Sponsored content must be technically relevant to Kubernetes operators. All sponsored entries are clearly labeled and held to the same structure standards as non-sponsored pages.

## Typical Engagement Flow

1. Share your product or role details by email.
2. Confirm fit, audience, and format.
3. Review final copy for factual accuracy.
4. Publish to site and newsletter slot.
